Game Rules

  • The objective of the game is to guess the hidden word by selecting letters.
  • You can guess until the whole body is formed, or 7 incorrect guesses.
  • Each correct letter guessed will be revealed in its respective position(s) in the word.
  • If you guess a letter that is not in the word, it will be an incorrect guess.
  • The game ends when you either guess the word correctly or run out of incorrect guesses.

And no cheating! But more importantly, Have fun :)
